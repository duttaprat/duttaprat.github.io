WHAT I DO

RESEARCH INTEREST

Protein Function Prediction

Developing deep learning-based multi-modal architecture to predict protein functions by utilizing the protein structure and protein sequence along with protein interaction information which has been converted into a web-based application.

IMPROVING Gene Clustering

Exploited Multi-objective Optimization technique(MOO), Ensemble technique, Generative models, Protein Interaction information for improving gene clustering, which is the primary step to understand the gene characteristics, hub gene prediction, etc.

Computational Model for Multi-omics Data

Developing various deep multi-modal(MM) and multi-task learning(MTL) framework for providing feasible solution for disease gene prognosis, biomedical text mining, and relation extraction from the clinical text.

AI for COVID-19 Solutions

Developing Artificial Intelligence-based models to tackle many aspects of the COVID-19 crisis at different scales including COVID-19 diagnosis from medical imaging, estimate the structure of SARS-CoV-2-related proteins and creating COVID19 Knowledge Graph.
